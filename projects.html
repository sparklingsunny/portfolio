Projects




  • Self watering plant pot


    • A prototype of a self watering plant pot that measures the amount of water in the water container, checks soil moisture for the plant and the amount of light the plant gets. It also has a separate module that measures the temperature and moisture of the air in the room. This can be connected to multiple plant pots in the room to accomodate any plant with different care needs. My role in this project was on the design part, which also included thinking of practical aspects of usage.
    Participants of the project: Helena Skwarczyńska, Jesper Prins, Olivier Spieker, Thijs Bolhaar, Ties Webers, Krzysztof Kabaciński.


















  • Japanese house 3D model

    • A 3D model of a Japanese house modeled in Blender. In this project the focus was on using the newly learned skills on texturing and modelling in practice.











  • Leonardo Da Vinci canon 3D model







    • A 3D model of a canon, based on Leonardo DaVinci sketches. In this project the focus was on imagining certain parts that weren't visible and making our own interpretation.





  • Bird house


    • A 3D model of a house that was made in fusion 360 and then transferred to blender to create a short animation and add textures to the surfaces to make them look more realistic.











  • AI cooking assistant


    • A project for a client aiCall. The main goal of this project was to reimagine the use of our client's Ai and create a completely different use case. As a group we decided to make an AI cooking assistant bot that would help young adults that have just started living on their own. It would also help with reducing the amount of waste, by providing the user with a recipe containing diverse ingredients.
    Participants of the project: Helena Skwarczyńska, Stella Tzertzeveli, Loek Apenhorst, Luis Alvares Diez del Corral, Xuanhe Chen.
